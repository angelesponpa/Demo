Biden awards Pope Francis with the Presidential Medal of Freedom with distinction

By. Angeles Ponpa

President Joe Biden awarded the Presidential Medal of Freedom with distinction, the nation's highest civilian honor, to Pope Francis on Saturday.

Biden bestowed the award on the pope during a phone call, after originally being scheduled to be presented to the pope in person in Rome during Biden's final overseas trip.

Source: Kevin Lamarque/Reuters, FILE

In a statement to NPR, the White house commended Pope Francis for his commitment to serving the poor, as well as advocating for peace and protecting the planet.

"Pope Francis, your humility and your grace are beyond words, and your love for all is unparalleled.", Biden posted on X.

Other recipients of the Presidential Medal of Freedom this year include:

  • Ralph Lauren
  • Lionel Messi
  • William Sanford Nye
